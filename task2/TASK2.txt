Recruiting the right candidate is often a time-consuming and complex
 process for organizations. HireIQ aims to simplify and improve this process
 through an intelligent, automated recruitment system powered by a multi-agent
 framework. In this system, different agents work together to handle tasks such
 as resume screening, background verification, candidate ranking, interview
 scheduling, and communication with applicants. These agents operate under a
 Master Agent, which coordinates their activities to ensure everything runs
 smoothly. By combining artificial intelligence with Large Language Models
 (LLMs), HireIQ can understand job requirements, evaluate candidates more
 accurately, and adapt to various hiring needs. This approach helps reduce
 manual effort and human bias while speeding up the entire recruitment cycle.
 Designed to be flexible and scalable, HireIQ brings transparency, efficiency, and
 fairness into the hiring process, showing how intelligent systems can make
 recruitment more effective and candidate-friendly.
